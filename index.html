About Us

Committed to Professional Security Training

Building careers. Protecting communities.

Zincume Security Services is a dedicated security training provider serving South Africa. We believe every security professional deserves access to quality, structured training that is both affordable and industry-relevant.

All training follows a Monday to Friday schedule, providing structure and consistency that helps students succeed. We are committed to clear communication, transparent pricing, and issuing certificates in a timely manner.
